All on dark backgrounds — picking the brand's primary energy and personality signal.

AI Strategy & Automation
Indigo primary · Cyan secondary · Navy/obsidian base

Indigo → Cyan

Classic premium AI palette. Indigo anchors brand credibility; cyan adds forward-looking tech energy. Think Vercel, Linear, Anthropic. The most trusted B2B AI color space right now.

Operational Transformation
Violet primary · Emerald secondary · Deep dark base

Violet → Emerald

Richer, more distinctive. Violet signals innovation and vision; emerald adds growth and operational energy. Less common than indigo — stronger differentiation from the AI pack. More personality, slightly bolder.

Strategic Intelligence
Blue primary · Gold accent · Deep navy base

Electric Blue → Gold

More enterprise-premium than the others. Blue signals trust and stability; gold signals premium quality and value. Feels more like a high-end consultancy than a pure tech company. Distinctive and bold.
